Career Back 2 Women

Get Back To Your Career

Forensics Intelligence Surveillance and Security Technologies (FISST) and Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IIT – M) is launching a program, “CareerBack2Women” aimed at women who wish to return to their career after taking a break.

If you have taken a break from your career for any of the following reasons:

  • Motherhood!
  • Relocating with your family!!
  • Caring for the elderly!!!

“Here is good news for such women technical professionals in the IT arena! “

  • Rebuild your career again after finishing your responsibilities.
  • Catch up with the latest technology changes and get back with capability & confidence
  • Realize your inherent potential and contribute to an organization

“CareerBack2Women opens the door for such motivated women “

THE CURRENT IT LANDSCAPE

CareerBack2Women (CB2W) is a bridge program that will help you catch up with the latest tools, techniques and technologies.

The new opportunities that have opened up are in Cloud, Mobility, Big Data, AI & Cyber Security that are current trends that are rapidly changing the IT industry.

THE MODULES TO PROPEL YOU INTO A REFRESHED CAREER PATH

“On successful completion IIT Madras will provide a certification.”

  1. CB2W gives specialized courses with basic and advanced tracks on current trending topics.
  2. The 20-hour foundation module ‘BTCUBE’ (Back To Technical Track) common to all entrants.

On clearing BTCUBE, four tracks, divided into basic and advanced will be available to choose from.

  • Data Science and Big Data Analytics
  • Artificial Intelligence / Machine Learning
  • Cyber Security
  • Software Engineering
  • Data Science and Big Data Analytics – Basic
  • Data Science and Big Data Analytics – Advanced
  • Artificial Intelligence / Machine Learning – Basic
  • Artificial Intelligence / Machine Learning – Advanced
  • Cyber Security – Basic
  • Cyber Security – Advanced
  • Software Engineering – Basic
  • Software Engineering – Advanced

HOW IT WORK

CB2W is a well-structured program that covers important current technology trends.

  • Teaching by industry’s best and most experienced faculty
  • Faculty with a mix of Academic and Industry professionals
  • Completely online course delivery with “Live” classes in the week-ends
  • Recorded sessions for recapitulating and learning at one’s own pace
  • Online assessments using MCQ
  • Internship provided for selected candidates who excel in the courses
  • Certification issued by IIT-Madras
